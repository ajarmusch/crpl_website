Hello I’m Sunita Chandrasekaran

Associate Professor at the Dept. of CIS, University of Delaware and this is my research group. Our research intrests include programming accelerators (GPUs), exploring the suitability of high-level prarallel programming models such as OpenMP and OpenACC for current and future platforms, developing solfware programming stack for embedded systems using industry standards such as Multicore Association (MCA) and validating & verifying emerging directive-based parallel programming models.

Post Doc - Felipe Cabarcas

Felipe Cabarcas graduated as Electrical Engineering in 1999 from the Universidad Pontificia Bolivariana, Medellin, Colombia. He obtained a Master of Electrical Engineering in 2002 from the University of Delaware. And a Ph.D. in Computer Architecture from the Universidad Politécnica de Catalunya in 2010, working in the Barcelona Supercomputing Center. He has been professor of Electrical Engineering in the Universidad de Antioquia for more than 10 years. During the last 10 years he has been teaching digital design courses; and his research has focused mainly on Genomics, and Metagenomics.
